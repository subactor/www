# Plesk deployment

Upload the contents of this repository's root (`index.html`, `assets/`, `pl/`, `privacy.html`, `security.html`, `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `site.webmanifest`) to `httpdocs/`, keep `.htaccess` and `.well-known/`, enable TLS, route hello@subactor.com and security@subactor.com, then verify desktop, mobile, OpenGraph, CSP and security.txt. The site is static.
